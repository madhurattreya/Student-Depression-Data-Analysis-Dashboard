# Student Depression Data Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze factors associated with student depression, academic stress, financial stress, study habits, and sleep patterns. The dashboard helps identify key behavioral trends and provides actionable insights for improving student well-being and academic performance.

## Key Business Objectives

* Analyze factors contributing to student depression
* Measure the impact of academic pressure on students
* Evaluate financial stress levels across the student population
* Examine study satisfaction and study hour patterns
* Understand the relationship between sleep duration and student well-being
* Provide data-driven insights for educational institutions

## Dashboard Insights

### Academic Pressure Impact

Academic Pressure Level 3 recorded the highest student count, indicating a significant concentration of students experiencing moderate academic pressure.

### Financial Stress Analysis

Financial Stress Level 1 showed the highest student count, suggesting that a large portion of students experience lower levels of financial stress.

### Study Satisfaction

Study Satisfaction Level 4 recorded the highest student participation, indicating generally positive academic experiences among students.

### Sleep Patterns

Students sleeping 7–8 hours and more than 8 hours represented the largest groups, highlighting healthier sleep habits within the dataset.

### Study Habits

The highest student count was observed among students reporting zero study hours, revealing significant variation in study behavior across the population.
